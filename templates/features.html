Overview

If you've ever purchased a new cell phone plan, you probably saw that there are a lot of different options. Carriers are always changing the options they offer and complicating the process of finding the plan that best fits your needs. What's worse, the plan that is best for you may be completely different from that which is best from your friends or online reviewers, making any reviews of carriers completely useless.

Here at RightCall, we recognize that cell phone plans are personal, but that finding them is complicated. Our goal is to simplify this process. We're removing all of the excess information that carriers throw at you and providing you with simple recommendations of exactly what you should do to maximize the bang for your buck.

Of course, we won't stop you from doing your own research, but we've already done the research, so we have the information and your time is much too important to be spent trudging through mountains of data about your cell phone usage and plan options from different carriers. So why not let us do it. We also have the experience and technology so that we can find your results quickly.

Our Process

Our process starts when you submit your information. We start by collecting and consolidating all of the information that we can use to predict your future usage. Once we have this, we look through our database of current plans. Based on many data points, we compare these plans to see which ones fit best with your usage. We then look at prices to provide you with the most economical options without limiting your phone use.

Where You Save

There are many things that cell phone companies do to rope you into paying more than necessary. While there are many little things that you can do to save a few dollars on your plan, we focus on the big picture: talk, text, and data. It is in our nature to want to avoid overage charges, but sometimes the occasional overage charge is cheaper than a consistently higher price. By using math, we are able to look at this without any personal bias.

Talk. Many companies offer several different quantities of minutes, but these only apply at certain times and sometimes they have different limits depending on whether you call during the day or at night. We can analyze all of these different factors to make sure that you are not spending more than you should.

Text. For texts, you often have the option of whether you want to pay as you go or buy texts up front and we do the math in our calculations to figure out which option is more apropriate to your usage.

Data. This is the place that most people overspend. It is hard to estimate how much data you use and it depends on many factors like whether or not you are using wifi or not. We look at the hard data which helps us to make the most informed possible decision.
